About Qarze Hasana

Qarze Hasana is an Islamic concept of providing interest-free loans to those in need. The Dhaka University Dawah Circle manages this system to help fellow students during financial difficulties.

All loans are interest-free and based on mutual trust. We believe in supporting each other as a community.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply?

Any current Dhaka University student with a valid @du.ac.bd email address.

How much can I borrow?

Students can request loans up to 3,000 BDT.

Is there any interest?

No! All loans are completely interest-free (Qarze Hasana).

How long do I have to repay?

The standard repayment period is 60 days from disbursement.
