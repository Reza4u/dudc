How It Works

Getting financial support shouldn't be complicated. We've made the process simple and dignified.

1. Create Account

Register using your institutional email (@du.ac.bd) and verify your identity securely.

2. Apply for Loan

Submit a simple application stating your need. No collateral required, just two witnesses.

3. Receive Funds

Once approved, receive funds directly to your mobile banking account (Bkash/Nagad).

About Us

Empowering Students, Building Future

**DU Qarze Hasana** is a student-run initiative managed by the **Dhaka University Dawah Circle**. Our mission is to provide interest-free financial assistance to students facing temporary hardships.

  • 100% Interest-Free (Shariah Compliant)
  • Managed by Students, for Students
  • Transparent & Dignified Process

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the loan process.

Any current student of Dhaka University with a valid registration number and institutional email is eligible.
Currently, the maximum loan limit is 3,000 BDT per student.
The standard repayment period is 60 days (2 months) from the date of disbursement.
